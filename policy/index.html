FAQ

How do I join a session?

Sign in with your Google account, browse the sessions on the home screen, and tap a session to open it. If there are spots available, tap Join →. Free sessions are confirmed immediately; paid sessions redirect you to a secure Stripe checkout page.

How do payments work?

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. You'll be redirected to a Stripe-hosted checkout page where you can pay by card. Roots never stores your card details. Session fees are set by the session holder and displayed before you confirm.

Can I get a refund if I can't make it?

Yes — if you cancel more than 24 hours before the session starts, you'll receive a full refund of the session cost (excluding any booking fee). Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. To cancel, open the session in the app and tap Cancel my registration.

How do I become a coach or host?

Open your profile, go to the Membership section, and tap Request → next to Coach or Host. An admin will review your request and notify you by email. Host accounts also require completing a Stripe onboarding to receive payments.

What happens if a session is cancelled?

You'll be notified by email. If you paid, a full refund including any booking fees is issued automatically to your original payment method and typically appears within 5–10 business days.

How do I join the waiting list?

When a session is full the Join → button changes to Join waiting list →. Tap it and you'll be added. If a spot opens up you'll receive an email — spots are filled on a first-come, first-served basis and are not held, so act quickly.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe Payments Europe Ltd, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified provider. Roots never sees or stores your card number. Your personal data is stored in Google Firebase under strict access controls.
