Levels

Our levels exist to keep every session safe and fun for everyone on court. Be honest about your ability — if you sign up above your level you may be asked to leave without a refund. Good sportsmanship is expected at every level.

Pre-approval required for Competitive and Elite sessions. Attend a Rep or Clinic to get rated first.
Beginner Recreational

New to volleyball or have only played casually for a short time. Rules and technique are still a work in progress. These sessions focus on learning and getting comfortable on court.

ServingCan get the ball over from the baseline (any style)
PassingAims toward the setter zone; occasional aces expected
SettingCan bump or hand-set a good pass toward an attacker
AttackingUnderstands the approach; attack is not a safety hazard
RotationsComfortable in social rotation; learning 5-1 / 6-2
Intermediate Recreational

You play regularly and are comfortable on court. You understand the game, know your way around rotations, and can perform the core skills of your position with reasonable consistency.

ServingOverhand from the baseline, in 7–8 / 10 times
Passing7 / 10 passes in the setter zone; rarely aced
SettingCan hand-set to outside or opposite on good passes
AttackingConsistent approach; ball in 8 / 10 attempts
RotationsUnderstands 5-1 / 6-2; in the right spot 8 / 10
Advanced London League Div 2–3

Experienced player who knows the game well. Comfortable with 3-touch play and all position responsibilities. May not have played on an organised team, but plays consistently and understands tactics.

ServingConsistent overhand; some precision on placement
Passing8 / 10 in-system; can handle jump floats
SettingSets middles and pins; understands tempo concepts
AttackingMultiple swing types; aware of blockers
BlockingLines up on power swings; not a safety hazard
DefenseGood positioning; handles most free balls cleanly
Competitive London League Div 1 Pre-approval required

Played on an organised team with coaching. Strong fundamentals, a clear positional role, and some advanced skills (jump serves, combination plays). You read the game and adapt when things go wrong.

ServingConsistent jump float or topspin targeting opponents' weaknesses
Passing5 / 10 in-system regardless of serve type
SettingRuns an offense with a game plan; can fix bad passes
Attacking25 %+ efficiency; multiple locations and swing types
BlockingEffective line and cross; solid 1v1 capability
Defense30 %+ dig rate; reads shifts and makes adjustments
Elite Première · Superleague Pre-approval required

College, club, semi-professional or international experience. Exceptional volleyball IQ and the physical ability to perform at a high level for the full session. Highest-level players in the UK.

Serving9 / 10 float, 7 / 10 topspin to intended location; advanced techniques
Passing60 %+ in-system; commands reception; covers more than a third of the court
SettingAdapts game plan in real time; all combinations; fixes bad passes consistently
Attacking30 %+ efficiency; all court locations with power and accuracy
Blocking1+ block / set; line, seam, cross, hard cross, split block
Defense40 %+ dig rate; chases every ball; excellent pattern recognition

Not sure where you fit?

Come to one of our open Rep or Clinic sessions. A coach will watch you play and advise the right level — and it's the path to pre-approval for Competitive and Elite sessions.
